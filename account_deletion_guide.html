Delete Your Account

PartyShare V2 allows you to permanently delete your account and remove all associated data directly from the app.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the PartyShare app.
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the bottom navigation menu.
  3. Select Account Info from the list.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the action when prompted. This will delete your account and all data.

What Happens Next?

Once deleted, your profile, check-ins, posts, and event data will be removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
