Privacy Policy for PartyShare V2

Last updated: May 26, 2025

PartyShare V2 ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and use the following types of personal information:

  • Camera Access: We use your camera to scan barcodes (PDF417) on government-issued IDs for the purpose of identity verification. We do not store or transmit full image data without your permission.
  • Location Data: Used to show events near your location and allow real-time check-ins.
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore: We store user profile information including name, email, and demographic data, which is used to personalize your experience and show relevant event content.
  • Firebase Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to help us improve app performance and features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify your identity
  • Show nearby parties and allow location-based check-ins
  • Send important account or update notifications
  • Improve user experience and app performance

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with Firebase (Google) for authentication and analytics. All data is handled securely using industry standards.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

PartyShare V2 is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to your data, correction of any errors, or deletion of your account by contacting us.
